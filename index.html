    Jul 2022 - Now
    University of Southern California

    The growth and characterization of monolayer MoS2 and carbon nanotubes

    Using CVD method to gorw monolayer MoS2 nanosheets, The effects of S precursor and pressure on growth were found.

    It is found that the concentration of S has a crucial impact on growth. Too high concentration of S will make MoS2 on the substrate more inclined to island growth. Since the base is buckled above the quartz boat, too low pressure may cause MoS2 to attach less to the base. And because of the gas expansion caused by heating, I also need to control the heating rate and the maximum temperature, and mechanical pump to control the gas pressure.

    The samples were characterized by Raman and AFM.
